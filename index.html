Blockchain Genesis: Bitcoin

Blockchain was invented by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2008 to serve as the public transaction ledger of the cryptocurrency bitcoin. The bitcoin design has inspired other applications; blockchain structure is expanding in areas like healthcare, government, education, logistic and real-estate.
